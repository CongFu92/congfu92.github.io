About

I am a researcher and engineering leader working at the intersection of machine learning research and real-world AI products. My work focuses on building efficient, scalable, and production-ready AI systems for recommendation, retrieval, ranking, search, and intelligent agents.

I currently serve as a Senior Research Scientist and Team Manager at Shopee Singapore, where I lead machine learning engineering work for large-scale e-commerce recommendation and merchandise ranking.

I also conduct independent academic research and collaborations outside my employment, subject to applicable conflict-of-interest, intellectual-property, and publication policies.

Research Interests

Recommender Systems and Generative Recommendation

I am interested in large-scale recommender systems, transformer-style recommendation models, semantic ID tokenization, user behavior modeling, multi-task learning, ranking optimization, and the integration of foundation models into personalized search and recommendation.

LLMs, RAG, and Agent Systems

I study efficient pre-training and post-training, retrieval-augmented generation, tool-use optimization, agent-style modeling, and scalable systems that combine foundation models with search, recommendation, and external tools.

Vector Search and Information Retrieval

I have long-standing interests in approximate nearest neighbor search, maximum inner product search, graph-based vector indexes, large-scale vector databases, and retrieval systems for both classical machine learning and modern AI applications.

Efficient and Practical AI Systems

I care about methods that can survive real-world constraints: scalability, latency, continual adaptation, model compression, distillation, training efficiency, and robust deployment in industrial-scale environments.

Research Collaboration

I am open to academic collaborations on topics related to efficient LLM training, recommender systems, large transformer-style recommender models, AI agents, retrieval systems, and vector search.

Current topics of interest include scaling laws for recommendation models, agent-style modeling for recommender systems, semantic ID learning for generative recommendation, RAG evaluation, and the integration of foundation models into personalized product search and ranking.

Background

I received my Ph.D. and Bachelor's degrees from Zhejiang University. My academic training spans machine learning, information retrieval, data mining, and scalable AI systems.

Before Shopee, I worked on large-scale AI systems and recommendation technologies at Alibaba Group. I have also conducted research on machine learning and knowledge representation as a visiting scholar at the University of Southern California.
